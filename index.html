    Our group affiliates in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, University of Houston. Our interests lie in creating new devices and technologies and understanding fundamental mechanisms, to solve some grand challenges in or related to healthcare, medicine, robotics, etc. Our research is carried out through combined efforts in manufacturing technologies, materials engineering, mechanical design, and electronics and sensors development.
