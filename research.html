Current research directions

  • Devices: Soft (flexible, stretchable, wearable) sensors and electronics, Thin film organic and inorganic electronics, Biomedical sensors and electronics, Soft robotics
  • Manufacturing: Nano-micro manufacturing, additive manufacturing
  • Materials: functional electronic materials, smart functional materials
  • Radiative properties: tunability, nonreciprocity, topology, and beyond
  • Mechanics: Mechanics of solids and soft materials, mechanical design
