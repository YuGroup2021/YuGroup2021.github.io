Openings

We have positions for Postdoc, PhD and visiting students/scholars.

  • Postdoc position: Candidates with background mentioned below are very welcome to directly inquire the position.
  • Ph.D. position: Self-motivated and hard-working students are encouraged to apply. Candidates with background mentioned below are very welcome to join my group. Please apply our PhD program and mention your interest in our research.
  • Undergraduate researcher positions: My group is active in recruiting undergraduates to involve in frontier interdisciplinary research, to enhance their further graduate school applications as well as industry career preparations.
  • Visiting Students/Scholars: We are accepting visiting students (earning degree with UH or your home institutions) and scholars with similar engineering and science background to join our group.
    • The research projects are highly interdisciplinary. Postdoc and PhD candidates with prior educational training and research experience in at least one of the following areas will be considered as priority.
  • Materials and Chemistry: organic electronics; semiconductors; inorganic electronics; Polymers synthesis
  • Electronics and Electronic materials: microelectronics, optoelectronics, solid state electronics (TFTs, detectors) sensors, device physics
  • Biomedical Engineering: tissue engineering, electrophysiology, Biomedical electronics; implantable devices
  • Mechanical Engineering: Robotics, soft robotics, advanced manufacturing
  • Solid mechanics: mechanics of thin films and soft matters
